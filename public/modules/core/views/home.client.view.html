Atlantic Motorsport Karters Association has been Nova Scotia's outdoor kart racing choice for 27 years. From May through to late September, rain or shine you will find us tearing up the track at Scotia Speedworld on Saturdays.

AMKA offers Divisions for racing enthusiasts from the age of 7 and up. Boys and Girls, Men and Women. If you share a passion for racing and having fun, you are welcome here.

We are always looking for new drivers and are very willing help new members get 'geared up'. Give us a call, send an email, or check out the schedule and come out to the track!
